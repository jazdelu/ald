About "A Little Dream"

”a little dream” is a shoe and accesories brand for babies and toddlers.

“a little dream” shoes gathers all the qualities a boy or girls needs in a first steps shoe. Soft and light, the shoes give freedom of movement, flexibility and balance for unequalled comfort.

Every shoe is hand-made using non-chemical tested leather which is sown to an impeccable finish. The final product provides our little ones with a fashionable and comfortable look!

About Alejandra

Everything is created lovingly by Alejandra, a Latin American designer and mother of two boys with over a decade of fashion experience in New York, Mexico and London.
